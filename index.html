About Our Team

Our team, Pangolins In Disguise, was founded in 2025 with a mission to inspire young people to be scientific and technological leaders. We participate in the FIRST Tech Challenge (FTC), where we design, build, and program a robot to compete in a head-to-head challenge. More than just robots, we are focused on developing valuable life skills like problem-solving, collaboration, and community outreach.
